Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. As co-founder and CEO of Apple Computers, Steve brought us the first personal computer, the ipod and the iphone.

Steve Paul Jobs is the full name of Steve Jobs. He was born on February 24, 1955 in California, United States. Steve Jobs as we know him is the founder of Apple Computer. He is a great figure with his extraordinary work in the field of technology. At birth Steve initially had the name Abdul Lateef Jandali until it changed when he was adopted by Reinhold and Clara Jobs. When Steve was a toddler the couple took him to Santa Clara Country, then known as Silicon Valley, where they also adopted a baby girl named Patti. This ability that Steve Jobs has developed quite rapidly while attending Cupertino Junior High School and Homesteat High School in Cupertino. It was there that he also met Steve Wozniak, who had the same interest as Steve Jobs in electronics. In 1976, Steve Jobs together with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne tried to establish his first Apple called Apple Computer Co, by receiving funding from product marketing manager and also semi-retired Intel AC technician, namely Mike Markula Jr. before finally establishing Apple together. .
